Common Questions

FAQ

How quickly will I get a reply?

Typically within 24–48 hours on school days. During exam season, response may be quicker. Weekends may have slightly longer delays.

Can I send a photo of my question?

Currently this form supports text only. Please type out or describe the question as clearly as possible. You can email directly for image attachments.

Is there a limit on how many doubts I can ask?

No limit — ask as many as you need. Each doubt is answered individually. Clearer questions get better, faster answers.

What if I need help with a full program?

Share the program question and explain what part is confusing. The teacher will guide you step by step rather than simply giving you the code — so that you genuinely learn.
